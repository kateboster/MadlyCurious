About...

Madly Curious, Inc. is a non-profit corporation established for the purpose of bringing large scale whimsical paper creations to long-term-stay hospital patients.

These bring into focus the communal love and support that we have for one another as we offer these symbols of life's kindness with a heartfelt and symbolic "thank you". Thank you for sharing your light, your personality and enriching our lives as we shared life's lessons together.

The desired goal is to grow this into a larger garden of paper blooms and other whimsical delights to be built with the combined efforts of artists, art therapists, current patients and survivors alongside their families and friends.
