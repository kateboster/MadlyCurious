Mary's Gold

This large flower was created for a young mother (33 yrs old) with 2 little ones. She had been in the hospital with a terminal diagnosis. With the help of the art therapists, I was able to bring this into her hospital room as a symbol of life's kindness. It was a welcomed surprise of joy as we wheeled this into her room, she expressed "This is life itself!". This now stands in her home with her family.

This was made with tremendous love and designed so that her family could have the flowers hang on the wall as an alternate way of sharing this display. She will be missed, she was such a bright soul.
